Who am I?

I am many things, good and bad, but for this purpose I will talk about one of the most important parts of my life - the artistic experience.
Since I was little, I've always had the impulse to create something with my emotions. Whether I felt nervous or peaceful, happy or sad, overwhelmed or apathetic, something pushed me to pick up a camera, capture the world around me and shape those few minutes into something that resonated with the emotions felt at that moment.
I always felt that the camera was an extension of me and that's why I intend to make my life around this world. To achieve this, I thought it would be appropriate to take a degree in Communication Sciences, in the Multimedia field at the Faculty of Arts and Humanities of the University of Porto and try my best to perfect my technique with a film camera.
There is never enough learning and that is why I feel that there is always something new to learn, whether in technique or in the form of expression.
In my free time, I like to make short videos that convey simple, everyday ideas.
At the moment, I am finishing my degree and I am excited about the challenges that I will have to face in the future within this area of ​​Multimedia.
I hope to be able, more and more, to carry out more complex and meaningful work that makes the person who experiences it so captivated that it makes them get up from the couch and take a reflective walk about the value of our existence... ok, no need all of that, but at least be able to take with you a little bit of what you took from these projects.
My name is Margarida Costa and this is a portrait of my mind. Feel free to explore this little world! Enjoy!
